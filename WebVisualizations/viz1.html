Max Temperature

This scatter plot illustrates the relationship between latitude and maximum temperature across cities. Latitude and maximum are strongly correlated, as illustrated by how close together the points are. This is expected because it shows weather becomes warmer as one approaches the equator. This is a nonlinear relationship.
